1. **Introduction** — Briefly explain the project
2. **Startup** — Show `python main.py` running
3. **Voice Input** — Speak a question into the mic

6. **Wrap-up** — Show the logs folder / conversation log

## Recording Tips

- Use [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/) or Loom for screen + audio recording
- Speak clearly and slowly during the demo
- Keep background noise minimal
